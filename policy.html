Privacy Policy for Volunteer Tracker App

Last updated: 6/20/2023

This Privacy Policy describes how the ChronoKeepr App manages personal information and respects your privacy. This policy applies to all users of our mobile application ChronoKeepr.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect any personal data of its own. However, it allows users (organizers) to manually enter and store information including names, email addresses, zip codes, and organizations on their own device. This data is entered at the discretion of the user and used solely for the purpose of tracking volunteers. We do not access, use, or share this data in any way.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on the user's device. We do not store, back up, or have any access to this data. While we aim to provide a secure environment for data storage on the device, we do not have control over the security measures on individual devices and cannot guarantee absolute security.
